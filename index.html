    Conformation

    Learn ensembles and pharmacophore-aware representations rather than treating a molecule as one frozen graph.

Research program

Representation with physical meaning.

My work asks how a model can preserve the geometry and dynamics that make molecules biologically active, while remaining useful for prediction and design.

01 · Represent

What should a molecular embedding remember?

Conformational-space and pharmacophore-aware learning creates representations that recognize shared activity even when two-dimensional scaffolds differ.

GeminiMol · molecular representation · scaffold hopping

02 · Navigate

Can a learned space become a route for design?

Unified encoders and decoders support property prediction, molecular migration, fusion, and directed exploration within one learned space.

Ouroboros · generative AI · chemical evolution

03 · Connect

How does molecular structure become biological evidence?

Protein-interface models and multimodal phenotype learning connect structural hypotheses to mechanism, target, and cellular response.

PPI-Miner · PhenoModel · target discovery
